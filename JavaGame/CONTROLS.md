#####ENGLISH KEYBOARDS

W = MOVE UP
A = MOVE LEFT
S = MOVE DOWN
D = MOVE RIGHT

Q = QUIT
Z = TELEPORT (Only from second map to first map)

M = MUSIC
N = NEW NPC (Does nothing yet, just follows player)
K = REMOVE NPC

#####FRENCH KEYBOARDS (AZERTY)

Z = MOVE UP
Q = MOVE LEFT
S = MOVE DOWN
D = MOVE RIGHT

A = QUIT
W = TELEPORT (Only from second map to first map)

M = MUSIC
